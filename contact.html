What we can help with

Questions about how a feature works, problems or bugs, ideas and requests, and anything about your dishes or prices. We usually reply within 1–2 business days.

Your data & privacy

Menu Calculate keeps your dishes, ingredients and prices on your device only — there is no account and no user database. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Subscriptions & billing

Purchases and renewals are handled by Apple. To view, change or cancel your subscription, open the App Store → your account → Subscriptions. For billing or refunds, Apple Support can help directly; for anything else, just email us.
